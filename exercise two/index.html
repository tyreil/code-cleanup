Bio

Neil deGrasse Tyson is an American astrophysicist and director of the hayden Planetarium in New York. He grew up in the Bronx, and studied at Harvard University, the University of Texas at Austin. and Columbia University. He joined Hayden Planetarium as a staff scientist in 1994, and became director in 1996. He hosted the show Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey (2014), which succeeded Carl Sagan's Cosmos: A Personal Voyage (1980) and received ctitical acclaim.
